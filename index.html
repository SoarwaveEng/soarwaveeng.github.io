A non-invasive care system powered by an agentic AI that continuously learns each resident's unique behavioural patterns — detecting anomalies before they become emergencies.

Under the Hood

Built on the frontier of
RF & Artificial Intelligence

Our technology stack combines deep RF engineering expertise with state-of-the-art machine learning — creating systems that are not just smart, but genuinely autonomous.

01
FMCW Radar
Frequency-Modulated Continuous-Wave radar provides high-resolution range and velocity sensing — the physical foundation enabling through-wall detection and non-contact vital-sign monitoring.
02
Agentic Machine Learning
Purpose-built AI agents that operate autonomously in real-world RF environments — learning, adapting, and deciding without human intervention.
03
Biometric Signal Processing
Novel algorithms that extract unique human biometric signatures from reflected RF waves, enabling non-contact identification and health monitoring.
04
Cognitive Spectrum Engine
Intelligent spectrum sensing and management that understands RF context, optimizing usage and detecting anomalies in real time.
